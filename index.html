Spare Hearts

"Spare Hearts" is an animated short about a little character made out of bolts and his unexpected journey to find love. This short took about one month to make, with all stages of the animation completed by myself.

  • Software: Audodesk Maya, Adobe Premiere
  • Date: April-May 2014
  • Created For: Digital Animation (HON494)

Laser Cutting/Engraving

I designed 3D models using 123D Make and prepared them for laser cutting. I have laser cut models from cardboard and wood including soundwaves, a human head, an elephant, and a dodecahedron.

  • Equipment: 123D Make, laser cutter/engraver
  • Date: September 2014-present
  • Created For: Prototyping Dreams (AME194)

Rube Goldberg Machine Animation

This Rube Goldberg machine animation uses physics-based animation in order to create realistic movement and interactions. I had about two weeks to model, animate, and texture this scene, as well as to put together the final camera shots in post-production. Watch the Rube Goldberg Animation here.

  • Date: March 2015
  • Created For: Introduction to Animation (FMP294)
